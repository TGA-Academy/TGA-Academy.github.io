Women’s VIP: Fitness, Community, and Empowerment

Women’s VIP represents more than just a fitness class—it’s an initiative built on shared values, practical movements and a strong commitment to women’s empowerment. Born from the collaboration between Raven Grappling known for its modern take on a Martial Arts training environment and Thr1ve Martial Arts Systems an organization dedicated to enhancing everyday resilience—Women’s VIP is a community which supports growth and uplifts its participants.

The Vision Behind Women’s VIP

Both Raven Grappling and Thr1ve share a mutual belief that safety and personal development are not only about techniques, but also mindset, community and support. As such the Women’s VIP program merges these values into a single experience to provide participants with the tools to build strength and conquer their goals. The VIP in Women’s VIP is short for Valkyrie Initiate Program. In Norse mythology, Valkyries are female handmaidens who walked the battlefield and escorted fallen warriors to Valhalla. They are strong, fearless, and a powerful symbol of victory after rest. This combined with the imagery of Yggdrasil—the tree of life—is the core visual of Women’s VIP. Fitness is a lifestyle, and this program is built to celebrate women, their strength, hard work, and community.

Why This Collaboration Matters

The Women’s VIP program bridges the gap between physical training and mental empowerment while providing a safe and accessible space for participants to learn and grow. In the end it’s about equipping women with the confidence to thrive while being supported by a community that uplifts and inspires.

What’s Next?

Women’s VIP is a piece of a larger movement—a growing network of women who value strength, resilience, and community. Through this partnership, Raven Grappling and Thr1ve Martial Arts Systems are creating a space where fitness, community, and empowerment intersect to help women grow stronger together.

Meet the Team
