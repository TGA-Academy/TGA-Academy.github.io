The Vision

Women VIP was born from the collaboration between Raven Grappling—known for its modern take on a Martial Arts training environment—and Thr1ve Martial Arts Systems—an organization dedicated to enhancing everyday resilience. It merges the values of personal development, healthy lifestyle, safety, community and support into a single experience.

The Inspiration

At Women VIP, we believe in the power of movement. Our signature full-body workouts are intentionally designed to blend the precision of barre, the intensity of HIIT, the core-focused control of mat Pilates, and the empowering strength of martial arts—creating a truly unique, results-driven experience. This is fitness that sculpts, strengthens, and sustains. Welcome to movement with intention. Welcome to Women VIP.

Meet the Team

Afnan

Passionate about helping people feel strong, confident, and empowered through movement, Afnan is a personal trainer who believes fitness should be inclusive for everyone. With expertise in strength training, HIIT, and corrective exercise, she designs workouts that are both challenging and adaptable—ensuring every participant, regardless of fitness level, is supported and successful in their workouts.

Her approach combines proven training techniques with a deep commitment to mental well-being, making each class a space where women can build strength for life, boost energy, and enjoy the process. Drawing from her background in education and client-centered coaching, Afnan creates an inclusive, motivating environment that inspires lasting results.

Zoe

A certified personal trainer, fitness instructor, and yoga teacher with over 15 years of experience, Zoe thrives on helping people move better, feel stronger, and achieve real results. Having led classes in gyms, fitness studios, military training programs, and with competitive sports teams, she is an experienced professional who brings her results-driven approach to a wide audience.

Zoe’s classes are designed to be safe, well-balanced, and effective workouts that meet each student where they are. Her fun, interactive teaching style builds not just physical strength, but also confidence and resilience, creating an environment where every participant feels empowered to succeed.
